Our Story

We Care for Your Business Just Like Our Own

Jacob Warren

Founder

PexNet was founded in 2016 by Jacob Warren, a seasoned digital strategist who had previously worked for a leading marketing agency in the UK. During his time there, he saw most digital and SEO firms were mainly serving large enterprises that could afford their high-priced services. As a result, many small businesses (e.g. local attorneys or plumbers) were unable to access the kind of reliable SEO support they needed to compete.

That’s when a simple idea came into Jacob’s mind: what if he could build an agency that would provide the same level of SEO support that big companies paid fortunes for? So, he stepped out on his own and launched PexNet from a small workspace in Manchester.

It didn’t take long for Jacob to secure clients from the USA, the UK, Canada, Australia, and Europe. Soon, he realized he needed a team that could scale without losing the personal, niche-focused approach that made PexNet different. He reached out to his long-time friend

Md Shah Alam

CEO

Shah Alam, a skilled digital marketer based in Bangladesh. Shah had rich experience with local SEO markets - the perfect match for what the agency was becoming.

Together, they expanded PexNet into a UK–Bangladesh hybrid agency. Now, we’re a renowned SEO firm helping startups and SMEs dominate their local markets with niche-driven SEO strategies.

However, after years of growth and expanding our services, one thing hasn’t changed: PexNet is still built on relationships, transparency, and the belief that SEO should be accessible to businesses of all sizes, shapes, and industries.
